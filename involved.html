TIdDLe: get involved!

There are many ways to get involved in TIdDLe. They all start with registering to the group ;-).

Collaborations

If you are looking for potential collaborators on your Deep Learning projects, check out our members list for relevant expertise.
You can post and consult announcements for potential collaborations in our online forum.
You can also sign up to our GitHub group, and initiate new projects, repositories, etc.

Events, announcements

All events are potentially interesting to us, from the smallest meetup to the major conference. If you're unsure whether your event is relevant or not, don't hesitate to contact us.

There are different ways to disseminate your event (or any other announcement) to the TIdDLe group:

  • Use our online forum to announce any Deep Learning related conferences, seminars or hackatons, meetups, etc.
  • TIdDLe members can also post announcements directly to our mailing list (moderated by the list admins).
  • To advertise an official TIdDLe event on our web page, you can go to our GitHub page, edit the "events.html" page and then submit a pull request. If you're not comfortable with GitHub, and/or if you need assistance with your event, contact us.

Website

Want to contribute to improving our website? It's all on GitHub (except the member database). Fork it, change it, make it better, submit your pull request!
